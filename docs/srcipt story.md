CHARACTER BACKGROUND

Hilbert Hickman (20 Years Old)

Childhood Dream: Architect/Inventor (Conjuring Object from his Mind)

Hilbert was once an eccentric young man, full of wild imagination. He used to draw some weird yet interesting schematic for some sort of invention. He did it with his buddy, —, they’re like 2 pieces of the same coin, can’t be separated. You can say, Hilbert is the mind, the logical one, while his friend is the heart of the project, the creative one. his optimism is the reason Hilbert can continue his dream.

But, every good things must come to an end. His friend died on an accident 1 year ago. Now, hilbert, a shell of his former self, lost his will to continue his dream, spending most of his time shut off in his room. Although, he still has hid families, but no one never really can light up his spark like his friend can.
